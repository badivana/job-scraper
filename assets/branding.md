# Branding

## Purpose

To define the brand identity of the AI-Powered Job Finder, ensuring a consistent and professional appearance across all touchpoints.

## Contents

- Brand Voice
- Visual Identity
- Logo Usage
- Color Palette
- Typography
- Imagery Style

## Brand Voice

Our brand is **helpful, professional, and empowering**. We speak to job seekers as a knowledgeable guide that simplifies the job search process and boosts confidence.

## Visual Identity

The visual identity combines a modern, clean aesthetic with friendly accents to convey trust and approachability.

See `assets/color_palette.md` for the primary, secondary, and neutral colors with exact HEX/RGB values.

## Typography

See `assets/typography.md` for font families, weights, sizes, and line heights.

## Imagery Style

We use a mix of realistic photos (diverse professionals in work settings) and simple line illustrations (Heroicons style) to maintain a cohesive look. Images should be authentic, inclusive, and well-lit.

## Tone of Voice

- **Helpful**: Provide clear instructions and actionable advice.
- **Professional**: Use correct grammar, avoid slang, and maintain respect.
- **Empowering**: Encourage users to take control of their career journey.
- **Concise**: Get to the point; avoid unnecessary jargon.

## Application Guidelines

Apply these guidelines consistently across:
- Web interface (Next.js app)
- Marketing website
- Email templates
- Social media profiles
- Presentation decks
- Documentation
